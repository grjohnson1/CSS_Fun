Dragon

The Chinese zodiac Dragon is the fifth animal in the zodiac cycle and symbolizes strength, confidence, and good fortune.

People born in the Year of the Dragon are often described as charismatic, ambitious, and innovative. The Dragon is also seen as a symbol of authority and is associated with positive traits such as being powerful, kind-hearted, and courageous. In Chinese culture, dragons are revered and considered auspicious, representing nobility and luck.

Monkey

The Chinese zodiac Monkey is the ninth animal in the zodiac cycle and symbolizes intelligence, adaptability, and innovative.

People born in the Year of the Monkey are often described as quick-witted, curious, and social. The Monkey is also seen as a symbol of playfulness with positive traits such as being entertainers, charismatic, and resourceful. In Chinese culture, monkeys are seen as curious individuals that bring wisdom, agility, and good luck.
